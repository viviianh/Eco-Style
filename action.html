EcoStyle

What can you do?

There are many things you can do to change your carbon footprint by just looking through your closet and being careful of what brands you buy from. You can also use your voice to create change by asking brands #whomademyclothes. By using your voice, work conditions for those who make your clothes will be safer and will be a stepping stone to progress

-Wear hand me downs

-Learn how to mend your clothes so they last as long as possible

-Ask yourself if you will really wear it repeatedly instead of just a few times

-Buy less but choose well made products

-If you want fresh clothes to style, swap with your friends!

-Don’t throw clothes away. Instead, donate them or if they are too torn up, repurpose them as a rag or use it to weave or knit!

-Buy clothes that are classics and you won’t want to discard due to it being no longer trendy.

-Thrift (resources below!)

-Buy from sustainable clothing brands (resources below!)

Where to shop:

Where you can thrift:
Click on any pin point and check out the thrift store near you! When thrifting, you are buying second hand clothes which contributes to the decrease in the wastefule textile production

Sustainable Brands:

“Become an active citizen through your wardrobe”. -Livia Firth- Founder and Creative Director of Eco-Age.

Pact

Comfy clothes made of cotton and monitored to make sure the process of making the clothes is as sustainable as possible.

For Days

A clothing brand where you buy their clothes and can continue swapping them out for the same piece of clothing for a discount. They ensure that the clothing is properly recycled.

Levi's

Jeans use a lot of water to make, so Levi's tries to decrease the amount of water used as much as possible, even having a collection that uses up to 96% less water to make.

Alternative Apparel

Focuses on using cotton and recycled materials, having strict ethical standards for their factories, more sustainable packaging, and less harmful dyes.

Everlane

Continuously proves that their factories are ethical and is very transparent. They offer classic styles that can be worn for years and years.
