What is sustainable fashion?

Sustainable fashion is a movement combating the unethical fashion industry. Unethical fashion pays many of their workers so little it barely covers their basic needs while they work in harsh conditions. It also causes pollution and excessive waste from clothes that are thrown away and produced. By addressing the problems within the fashion industry, we are fighting for environmental rights and human rights.
