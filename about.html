About Us

What we do

We provide information on how you can become a more eco friendly consumer in your community. There are many ways that you can avoid fast fashion as showcased in our website. We created this website to help people become more sustainable and ethically conscious in making decisions about their clothing. It is crucial to inform users on the importance of sustainable fashion and the damages done by the fashion industry.

img source: Megan Knapp

Why?

We hope to create a informative space where people can gain more knowledge about this movement and how to help the cause, raise awareness on this issue to also allow people to create change they want to see, and inspire people to start their personal eco friendly fashion journey.
